About Ladies' Hack Day

Ladies' Hack day is a day for women of all skill levels and ages to get together and build something radical. If you’ve *never* written a line of code in your life, but you want to learn, we want you here! If you’re a super awesome programmer who knows more about compilers than Grace Hopper, we’re honored to have you in our midst. We’re here to work together, supported by helpful people, sharing, and cupcakes. You’ll leave with a fully functioning project that you can call your own.

About Nashville Women's STEAM Group

We’re a collective of Scientists, Technologists, Engineers, Artists, and Mathematicians, aiming to end the leaky pipeline by sharing our support and our skills.

Why should I learn to program?

Because the future depends on your learning to program. Even if you only know a little bit, knowing more about how the internet works can open up opportunities for you. Programmers are paid *well* to solve fun problems all day, shouldn't more women and young women be joining in? We think so.

Programming is not scary. It is not lonely. It is not mathematical! We've created a safe space for you to ask questions and learn.

What should I work on?

  • An easy to follow beginner tutorial for HTML/CSS
  • An easy to follow beginner tutorial for modding Minecraft
  • An intermediate project tutorial featuring JS
  • An advanced tutorial featuring Python
  • Or, hack on your own!

I'm not a lady.

Well, barely are we! But we're redefining what it means to be a lady.

Guests of all genders are welcome, as long as male attendees are the personal guests of ladies.
