Ladies' Hack Day

May 11th, 10:00am to 6:00pm

All ages welcome

Ladies' Hack Day was built for you to learn how to build new projects on your own.

  • An advanced tutorial featuring Python

  • Your awesome self (and a friend!)
  • Sublime text editor downloaded on your computer
  • A browser
