About Ladies' Hack Day

Ladies' Hack day is a day for women of all skill levels and ages to get together and build something radical. If you’ve *never* written a line of code in your life, but you want to learn, we want you here! If you’re a super awesome programmer who knows more about compilers than Grace Hopper, we’re honored to have you in our midst. You’ll leave with a fully functioning project that you can call your own.

We'll provide coffee, tea, lunch, and cupcakes. You provide your awesome self.

About Nashville Women's STEAM Group

We’re a collective of Scientists, Technologists, Engineers, Artists, and Mathematicians, aiming to end the leaky pipeline by sharing our support and our skills.

Why should I learn to program?

Because the future depends on you. Even if you only know a little bit, knowing more about how the internet works can open up opportunities for you. Programmers are paid *well* to solve fun problems all day. Shouldn't more women and girls be joining in? We think so.

Programming is not scary. It is not lonely. It is not mathematical. We've created a safe space for you to learn and ask questions. We're here to start a revolution, be a part of it!

What should I work on?

  • We provide an easy to follow beginner tutorial for HTML/CSS
  • We provide an easy to follow beginner tutorial for modding Minecraft
  • We provide an intermediate project tutorial featuring JS
  • We provide an advanced tutorial featuring Python
  • Or, hack on your own!
